Binary CookBook

Binary CookBook is an interactive and useful web application that helps you discover and prepare the most convient meals for your lifestyle.


Are you in a hurry? Have you run out of ideas of what to cook? Or do you simply feel like trying something new and adventurous?
Well, then this app is for you. Just open the fridge, choose a number of ingredients and add them to this site and BCB will suggest what you can cook using them, as well as step by step cooking instructions.


This app was created using Python, Django and Scrapy during the mentorship program 'Learn IT, girl!' :)
